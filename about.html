About Dragon’s Den

Built for players, collectors, and friends who want a place to relax, compete, and be part of a welcoming gaming community.

Our Story

Dragon’s Den Gaming Café was designed to bring multiple parts of gaming culture into one space. The business combines a sit-down café, private tabletop gaming rooms, an arcade section, and a small shop for comics, collectibles, and hobby supplies. The goal is to create a place where customers can enjoy games, spend time with friends, and feel part of an active local community.

In addition to offering space for games like Dungeons & Dragons and Warhammer, Dragon’s Den also provides custom figurine painting and modification services. This gives customers a chance to personalize their collections while also enjoying events, tournaments, and themed game nights in a social and immersive setting.

What Sets Us Apart

Community First

The café is built around community interaction, shared interests, and creating an inviting place for both new and experienced players.

Specialized Spaces

Private rooms and organized play areas help support tabletop campaigns, casual matches, and hobby-focused events.

More Than a Café

Dragon’s Den blends food, games, collectibles, and custom services into a business model that offers more than a standard coffee shop or arcade.
