What We Offer

Dragon’s Den Gaming Café combines the atmosphere of a fantasy gaming lounge with the energy of a classic arcade. Whether you want to host a DnD campaign, play Warhammer, enjoy arcade machines, or commission custom-painted miniatures, there is a place for you here.

What We Offer

Private Game Rooms

Reserve dedicated rooms for Dungeons & Dragons, Warhammer, and other tabletop sessions with friends.

Arcade Floor

Enjoy a mix of classic and modern arcade machines in a bold, high-energy gaming environment.

Custom Figurines

Commission painted or customized miniatures for display, tabletop use, or collection pieces.

Café and Shop

Grab drinks and snacks while browsing comics, collectibles, and tabletop gaming accessories.
