Arcade Floor

From classic cabinets to modern favorites, the arcade section of Dragon’s Den adds a high-energy experience to the café.

Arcade Experience

The arcade floor is designed to give customers another way to enjoy their time at Dragon’s Den. Whether someone is waiting for a tabletop session to begin or just wants to stop in for a quick game, the arcade adds variety and excitement to the overall experience.

We want the arcade area to feel welcoming to casual players while still offering a fun atmosphere for people who enjoy competition, nostalgia, and social gaming.
